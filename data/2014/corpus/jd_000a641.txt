the department of english  at the college of charleston welcomes applications for a full-time, non-tenure-track  instructor position. ma in composition studies or in english  with training in composition studies required, phd  in composition studies desirable. the teaching assignment is typically 12 hours per semester (three 4-hour courses),  primarily in first-year writing (introduction to academic writing and interdisciplinary composition). instructors  undergo major reviews for promotion to senior instructor in their sixth year and, subsequently, for renewal every five  years and are full voting members of the department. founded in 1770 and located in the heart of historic charleston, the college of charleston is a public liberal arts and  sciences institution with a strong commitment to the teacher-scholar model for faculty. the college of charleston  is an affirmative action/equal opportunity employer  and encourages applications from all underrepresented groups.  submit cover letter, cv, statement of teaching philosophy, current transcripts, and three letters of recommendation by  dec. 20, through interfolio.
